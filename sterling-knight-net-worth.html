Sterling Knight Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Sterling Knight:

$2 million

Sterling Knight is an American actor. He had his career start in the mid-2000s, first debuting in an episode of Hi-Jinks. His first recurring role was as Lucas in Hannah Montana. After guest appearances in Grey’s Anatomy and The Closer, he landed the role of Chad Dylan Cooper in the Disney series Sonny with a Chance. After this, he reprised his role in So Random! He later had roles in the films 17 Again, Elle: A Modern Cinderella Tale, The Muppets, and Transit. He also starred in the TV movie StarStruck. In addition, Knight had recurring roles in The Hotwives of Orlando and Melissa & Joey.
