Megyn Kelly poses for a portrait in New York. Donald Trump was a guest on Kelly’s first Fox network special, which aired on May 17.

Megyn Kelly is a journalist who is known for her work on Fox News and NBC News. She began her journalism career in 2003 when she moved to Washington D.C. and worked as a general assignment reporter for WJLA-TV. She covered many major news events, including the retirement of Justice Sandra Day O’Connor and the 2004 presidential election. She went on to FOX News after applying in 2004. She hosted her own legal segment called ‘Kelly’s Court’ for ‘Weekend Live’ and sometimes filled in for Greta Van Susteren on her popular television program, ‘On The Record’. She progressed at the network by hosting her own shows, including ‘America Live’ and ‘The Kelly File’.

She started working for NBC News in 2017 and hosted her own show as part of the the network’s morning show, ‘Today’, but after revealing her beliefs about blackface during a discussion with guests on the show, Megyn’s show was cancelled.
