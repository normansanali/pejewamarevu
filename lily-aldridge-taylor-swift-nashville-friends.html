Lily Aldridge and Taylor Swift in New York

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Media Platforms Design Team

Lily Aldridge and Taylor Swift brought their blooming Nashville friendship to the streets of New York this week. The stylish pair were recently spotted taking a chic stroll around Tribeca. Aldridge looked like a city girl at heart in head-to-toe black, pairing her Rta denim with 3.1 Phillip Lim over-the-knee boots, while the girly Pop Princess dressed in a feminine polka dot dress. Alridge relocated to Swift's hometown of Nashville after marrying Kings of Leon frontman Caleb Followill in 2011, who is also a Tennessee native.

Chrissy Rutherford is a contributing editor at HarpersBazaar.com
