ʻO wai ʻo Alice Kim?

ʻO Alice Kim, he mea hoʻokani pila ʻAmelika, kaulana ʻo ia no nā kiʻiʻoniʻoni he nui a me nā moʻolelo kīwī. He waitress hoʻomaha ʻo ia i hoʻokani i nā kiʻiʻoniʻoni he nui e like me Next, Grindhouse. ʻO ia hoʻi ke kolu o ka wahine a Cage, ka mea i male i kahi hānai pilikino ma ka ʻākau o Kaleponi ma Iulai 30, 2004. Ua male lāua ʻelua ma Ianuali 2016. Ua hānau ʻia lāua he keiki kāne ʻo Kal-El, i hānau ʻia ma 2005.

ʻElua mea i kaulana ai ʻo Alice Kim.
