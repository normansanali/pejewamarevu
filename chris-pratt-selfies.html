You Won't Be Getting A Selfie With Chris Pratt Anytime Soon

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Many of us dream of getting that perfect selfie with one of our favorite celebrities one day, but unfortunately, you have to cross Chris Pratt of that list.

In a new interview with Entertainment Tonight the Guardians of the Galaxy star revealed that he isn't interested in taking selfies with fans anymore, following Emma Watson and other A-listers who have done the same.

Pratt confessed that he wants to be able to go out in public and "do normal things," which means he'll "have to be comfortable" disappointing fans from time to time.

Getty Images

Taking a selfie with fans at the premiere of ’The Magnificent Seven’ during the 73rd Venice Film Festival at Sala Grande.

"I just don’t take pictures with people. Because that’s not about enjoying the moment; it’s about stealing the moment to brag about later," he explained.

But even when he insists he and his fans have a more personal interaction, it doesn't go as planned. "So I say, ‘Would you settle for a handshake?’ And then they take the picture anyway,” he added.

We'll settle for that handshake for now, Mr. Pratt.
