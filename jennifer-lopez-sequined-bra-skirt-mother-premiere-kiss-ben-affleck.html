Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck Share Kiss at The Mother Premiere

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Mr. and Mrs. Affleck couldn’t stop showing off their love on the red carpet last night.

The couple attended the premiere of Jennifer Lopez’s most recent film, The Mother, held at the Westwood Regency Village Theatre in Los Angeles. They walked the carpet together, embraced, and shared kisses for the cameras.

For the occasion, Lopez wore a dazzling ivory ensemble custom-made by Brunello Cucinelli. The monochrome three-piece set was made up of a bra top, a floor-length skirt, and a long coat—all of which featured a subtle pinstripe pattern lined with silver sequins. She added another silver accent with a silky clutch from Tyler Ellis and wore her signature big hoops, though this time they were abstract pieces from Fernando Jorge jewelry, embellished with shimmering white crystals.

The singer opted for bronzy, glowy makeup and a half-up, half-down hairdo.

Husband Ben Affleck, next to her, looked dapper in a textured black three-piece suit, crisp white shirt, and black dress shoes.

The Mother will be available to stream on Netflix starting this Friday, May 12. In the action film, Lopez plays an assassin fleeing from dangerous assailants, who “comes out of hiding to protect her daughter she left earlier in life,” per IMDb.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The Afflecks’ attendance at the premiere is the most recent in a string of sweet, PDA-filled appearances for the couple.

This March, Lopez supported Affleck at the premiere of his film Air; while there, the two shared another red-carpet kiss. And back in February, the pair famously made their Grammys debut and looked so in love while getting ready for and sitting together at the ceremony (though Affleck later went viral for seeming hilariously bored).
