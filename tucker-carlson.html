Tucker Carlson began his journalism career shortly after he graduated from college with B.A. in history. Today, we know him as a conservative political commentator for Fox News.
In 2002, he quit drinking alcohol while, a few years earlier, he quit smoking and replaced it with nicotine gum that he chews constantly.

He has been married to his wife since 1991 and they share 4 children together.
