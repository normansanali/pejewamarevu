Matt Hardy Throws Subtle Jab at Sammy Guevara Amid Concerns Over Jeff Hardys Well-Being

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Matt Hardy remains one of the veterans in AEW right now and he is never afraid of speaking his mind on any matter online, especially regarding his company. Sammy Guevara possibly concussed Jeff Hardy recently and therefore, Matt Hardy decided to throw a subtle jab at Guevara on social media.

As we have reported earlier, Sammy Guevara competed against Jeff Hardy in a singles match during this week’s AEW Rampage tapings. Regrettably, the match has raised concerns about Hardy potentially sustaining a concussion, as Guevara’s knee landed on Hardy’s face during the bout.

Ahead of the bout airing on AEW television, Matt Hardy took to Twitter and uploaded a video of Sammy Guevara throwing a chair at Matt Hardy’s face back in 2020, which legitimately busted him open and lacerated him. It was evident he was throwing shade at Guevara with this move.

”#TBT 2020 I don’t forget”

It was also reported that Jeff Hardy underwent medical examination for a possible concussion. Further details regarding the incident have also been revealed. It remains to be seen what will become of Jeff Hardy in the end.
