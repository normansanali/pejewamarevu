Margot Robbie Has Debuted Curly Hair And It Looks Amazing

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Margot Robbie is no stranger to a major hair transformation. We've had slicked back ponytails, poker straight blowouts and her all-time signature, the blonde asymmetrical bob, ever since she hit the spotlight.

Just as the first rays of sunshine have begun to warm our faces, the Aussie beauty has switched up her sophisticated look for something more summer appropriate - and safe to say, she looks totally unrecognisable.

While promoting her new film, Dreamland, at the Tribeca Film Festival in New York City, the 28-year-old ditched her trademark blonde locks for a show-stopping set of spiral curls. Seriously, check out this volume!

We don't yet know who's behind this killer look, but Robbie's longtime hair stylist is Bryce Scarlett, who usually creates ultra sleek looks for the actress and Chanel ambassador's red carpet events. Scarlett hasn't posted a snap of this look though, which could well mean that Margot styled her hair herself #goals.

While we've grown accustomed to the I, Tonya star's polished looks, we have to admit we're living for this big, natural beauty. Coupled with those denim kick flares, and Robbie could have walked straight out of the 1970s.

If these are indeed Robbie's natural curls then a) we have major hair envy and b) it's time to call your hairstylist. Considering the spring season is upon us, it's the perfect time to debut a brand new look.
