Nate Diaz Chokes Out Logan Paul Look-Alike In Brutal Street Fight

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Logan Paul seems to be everywhere, even when it’s not really him. The Maverick recently went viral after Nate Diaz choked out a man on the street who looked a lot like the YouTuber turned WWE Superstar.

Nate Diaz isn’t someone that you want to mess with. He recently had an altercation with a man on the street, and he choked him out for good measure. This caused the blonde man to go crashing to the pavement.

This fight went down in New Orleans when Nate Diaz was there to support teammate Chris Avila, who was fighting on a Misfits boxing card at the XULA Convention Center.

That clip went viral, because it’s a fight video in 2023. A lot of people watched it, and some even mistook that knocked out foe as Logan Paul. Some of those people might be important.

Dana White actually thought it was Logan Paul at first. Adin Ross was playing poker with Dana White at the time, and he showed White the clip. This was quite a funny moment, which you can see below.
