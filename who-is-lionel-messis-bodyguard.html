Who is Lionel Messi's bodyguard?

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Lionel Messi, widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, has a personal bodyguard named Yassine Cheuko. Cheuko, an ex-soldier and former U.S. Navy SEAL, is responsible for ensuring Messi's safety both on and off the field. With Messi's recent move to Inter Miami in Major League Soccer (MLS), the attention and fan frenzy surrounding him have prompted the club to increase security measures, including the addition of Cheuko to the team.

The Enigmatic Yassine Cheuko

Despite his prominent role as Messi's bodyguard, Yassine Cheuko remains a mysterious figure. Little is known about him, and he has refrained from giving interviews or seeking attention away from his work. This anonymity has only added to his aura and mystique.

Cheuko's online presence is carefully curated, and he maintains a low profile, avoiding unnecessary attention. Fans have attempted to meet him by running onto the field during Inter Miami games, but Cheuko swiftly removes them, ensuring Messi's safety remains a top priority.

An Approachable Protector

Although Yassine Cheuko keeps a low profile, he is approachable and possesses a calm demeanor. This quality is essential in his role as Messi's bodyguard, as it allows him to handle situations with composure and ensure the football star's safety.

Recently, Cheuko demonstrated his dedication and protective nature when he shielded a young pitch invader from harm and then escorted the youngster to meet Messi for a special moment. This act showcased Cheuko's commitment to his role beyond just security, fostering a positive and memorable experience for the fans.

Enhanced Security Measures

With Lionel Messi's arrival at Inter Miami, the club has taken significant steps to ensure his safety. The attention and fan frenzy surrounding Messi necessitated an increase in security measures both on matchdays and during training sessions.

Inter Miami has added staff members to the security team, including Yassine Cheuko, who remains by Messi's side on the sideline during matches. Cheuko's presence provides reassurance and an added layer of protection for the football star.

In addition to personnel, Inter Miami has heightened security measures at their stadium and training facility, implementing additional fencing and measures to ensure a safe environment for Messi and the team. Other MLS teams have also increased security when playing against Inter Miami, recognizing the significance of protecting a player of Messi's stature.

Lionel Messi's personal bodyguard, Yassine Cheuko, plays a vital role in ensuring the safety and security of the football superstar. Despite his enigmatic and mysterious nature, Cheuko remains approachable and displays a calm demeanor while fulfilling his responsibilities.
