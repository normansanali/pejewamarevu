Meet Quincy Alexis, HGTV Page Turner Daughter

Meet Quincy Alexis, one of the three daughters of HGTV star Page Turner. She is very close to her mom and posts TikTok videos with her. Below you’ll learn about her age, her job, and her marital status.

  • Quincy Alexis Age

In September 2021, Quincy Alexis turned 25 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 4 September making her a Virgo.

  • Quincy Alexis Job

Quincy Alexis is pursuing a career as a fashion model. On her website, Quincy stated that she loves collaborating “with photographers and stylists and turning our ideas into reality”. She takes pride in her “unique look and a sweet personality that turns head everywhere she goes.”

Besides fashion modeling, Quincy has also done some modeling work in music videos in the song Bad Vibrations by Val Astaire, Let Me Know (LMK) by Boryan, Tinnmanproducer, and BCGOTBARS. Moreover, she also modeled in a music video of Devin The Ripper’s titled Pac Angeles.

Quincy is also a phenomenal artist who has done reproduction of Edvard Munch’s Scream that she posted on her Instagram. Besides her job, she also fights for the cause of social justices life Black Lives Matter, and Women’s Rights.

Talking about her academic qualifications, she graduated from Bellarmine University with a bachelor’s degree in arts.

  • Is Quincy Alexis Married?

No, Quincy Alexis isn’t married. She appears single as of March 2022.

Meet Qai Alexandria, HGTV Page Turner Daughter

Qai Alexandria is the sister of Quincy and one of the daughters of Page Turner from HGTV. She and her sister Quincy were born in Los Angeles, California, and were raised in Nashville, Tennessee.

  • Qai Alexandria Age

Because Qai is Quincy’s twin, she also turned 25 years old on 4 September 2021.

  • Qai Alexandria Job

Qai Alexandria Gordon is currently working as a Systems Analyst at Wellington Management starting from July 2018. She graduated from the University of Notre Dame and earned her BBA in Information Technology Management in 2018. She completed her high school at St. Cecilia Academy.

While in college, Qai interned at Braces by Dr. Ruth, United States Senate SAA, National Council of La Raza, and NAACP. She also worked as Student Ambassador and Note Taker at the University of Notre Dame, worked as Sales Associate at Journeys, and hostess at Logan’s Roadhouse.

Besides her job, she is also into modeling.

  • Is Qai Alexandria Married?

No, Qai Alexandria isn’t married yet. She also prefers to remain quiet about her dating life.

Meet Zaire Kizito, HGTV Page Turner Daughter

The eldest daughter of HGTV’s Page Turner is Zaire Kizito.

  • Zaire Kizito Age

Reportedly born in 1995, Zaire Kizito turned 26 years old in 2021. She celebrates her birthday on 2 April.

  • Zaire Kizito Job

According to Zaire Kizito’s LinkedIn, she is working as a Mortgage Specialist at AmeriSave Mortgage Corporation. She had previously worked as Healthy Kids Club Instructor at East Tennessee Children’s Hospital. Before that, Zaire worked as lead server/cocktail server at Seminole Casino Coconut Creek and also as Real Estate Assistant at eXp Realty with her mother.

Zaire is a graduate of Antioch St. Cecilia Academy of 2013. She graduated from the University of Tennessee with a BA degree in English Rhetoric and Writing in 2018.

Yes, Zaire Kizito recently married her husband Rodney Kizito. They met each other on 10 October 2017 in Bible Study as prayer partners. Rodney proposed to her in April 2021 which she announced on her Facebook, “Zaire Kizito has a ring to it”. They got married on 10 October 2021.

Zaire revealed on her Facebook, “We had a full 3 day wedding weekend full of party busses, karaoke, rooftops, and a yacht! It was the best weekend of our life and this is just the start. There is so much I can write yet so many words can never express. I love you so much my Instagramless husband, Dr. Rodney Moses Kizito. You are my absolute best friend and I am so excited for the beautiful life God planned for us.”

Rodney studied holds a BS degree from the University of Pittsburgh, an MS degree from the University of Kansas, and a Ph.D. from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in Industrial Engineering.
