Flume And Dillon Francis Added To Electric Forest Lineup

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Michigan’s Electric Forest is preparing for a massive outing in 2017. The festival announced its lineup last month, revealing a roster filled to the brim with enticing acts like Bassnectar, Nero, DJ Snake, Above & Beyond, ODESZA and many more. Joining the already stacked lineup as headliners are none other than Flume and Dillon Francis, two names sure to draw in the crowds. Festival goers will no doubt be pleased to see the two DJs added to the program for what is sure to be an unforgettable event.

Recommended Videos

The upcoming edition of Electric Forest will represent the first time the festival will be spread across two weekends. The first weekend will take place from June 22-25, while the second will run from June 29-July 2. Dillon Francis is set to play both weekends, while Flume will only appear over the second weekend.
