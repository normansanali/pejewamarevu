Trinity Cast In Queen Of The Ring Film

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Trinity has landed a movie role.

Trinity, formerly known as Naomi, shared the news that she has been cast in “Queen of the Ring,” a film that is based on women’s wrestling legend Mildred Burke.

Per Production List, the film’s story is as follows:

“The Queen of the Ring is the story of Mildred Burke, a waitress who attended her first wrestling match and became the longest reigning champion of female wrestling. She fought and beat male wrestlers, and walked the fine line between beauty and brawler.”

WWE stars Charlotte Flair and Liv Morgan are also reportedly part of the cast.

Trinity will be in action on the June 29 episode of IMPACT Wrestling; she will face Jai Vidal in a singles match. WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
