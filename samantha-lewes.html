Samantha Lewes (born November 29, 1952 – died March 12, 2002) was an American Actress and Celebrity Former Partner from San Diego, California. She is best known for being the first spouse of Tom Hanks (American actor and filmmaker).

He is one of the most celebrated Hollywood stars who first gained recognization for his films such as Bachelor Party, Splash, Turner & Hooch, etc. Furthermore, Tom even widely dubbed as an American cultural icon.

His performance in Steven Spielberg’s films named The Post, The Terminal, Bridge of Spies, etc also appreciated by fans a lot. Hanks is also a fifth-highest-grossing actor in the USA. Moreover, he also won prestigious awards including an Academy Award for his role in Philadelphia (1993).

Some of his other popular projects are Forrest Gump, Saving Private Ryan, Captain Phillips, A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood, Toy Story, You’ve Got Mail, Apollo 13, etc. He is the second actor who won Oscars for best actor in two consecutive years.

Marriage, Spouse Tom Hanks & Kids

In 1978, Samantha Lewes tied the knot with her husband “Thomas Jeffrey Hanks” after dating him for some time. Their wedding ceremony was a private that took place in the presence of close friends & family.

In fact, the internet has no information regarding Samantha and Tom Hanks’ marriage date and venue. Through their marriage, the couple welcomed two beautiful children. On 24 November 1977, Lewes gave birth to her first son named Colin Hanks (American actor, director, and producer).

Later, on 17 May 1982, their daughter “Elizabeth Ann Hanks” (actress) stepped on this world. However, Samantha and Thomas Hanks married life didn’t last long and they took divorce in 1987.

Later, the actor wedded his second wife “Rita Wilson” (American actress, singer, songwriter, and producer) in 1988. They also have two sons named Chester Marlon “Chet” Hanks and Truman Theodore Hanks.

On March 12, 2020, it was announced by Samantha’s ex-husband that he and his present wife were diagnosed by a coronavirus. They are getting their required treatments in Australia and currently doing fine.
