WWE Denies Brock Lesnar Report, Dean Ambrose and Bray Wyatt Battle After WWE Raw Tonight

Publish date: 2024-07-20

WWE Denies Brock Lesnar Report

Earlier today, Bryan Alvarez of F4WOnline.com reported WWE instructed its merchandise department to stop making new merchandise for Brock Lesnar, and the backstage feeling in WWE is Lesnar will be leaving the company when his current contract expires.

In response to Alvarez’s report, WWE issued the following statement: “There is not truth to this report.”

Dean Ambrose vs Bray Wyatt Takes Place After Raw

After WWE Raw went off the air tonight, Dean Ambrose and Bray Wyatt battled in the event’s dark segment, despite the two not appearing on the live Raw broadcast.

Ambrose and Wyatt brawled for awhile, fighting each other with both Kendo Sticks and chairs. Dean Ambrose eventually hit Dirty Deeds on Wyatt, and then he celebrated with the live crowd to end the show.
