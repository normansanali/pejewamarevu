Anna Jay Took Scary Bump During AEW Rampage Television Taping

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Anna Jay was a beloved babyface before she betrayed Dark Order and her fans to align herself with Tay Melo. Anna turned heel by helping Tay Melo against Ruby Soho on the July 20, 2022, episode of Dynamite. The duo crossed paths with Ruby Soho and Willow Nightingale last night on Rampage.

Click here to read the complete spoiler results for AEW Rampage.

Ruby Soho and Willow Nightingale teamed up to take on Tay Melo and Anna Jay in a Street Fight last night on Rampage. The match was taped after the conclusion of Wednesday Night Dynamite at the Kia Forum on January 11, 2023. At one point during the match, Anna took a scary bump.

The spot in question saw Anna Jay take a powerbomb from Willow Nightingale off of the stage and onto a table. Instead of landing on the table, however, Anna landed on the floor, her head bouncing hard upon impact. As of this writing, AEW hasn’t provided an update on Anna’s condition.

Ruby Soho and Willow Nightingale picked up the win after a brutal and bloody fight. Soho, who got busted open during the early moments of the match, got the win for her time after she hit Tay Melo with a Destination Unknown on a pile of thumbtacks.

As previously reported, the match received positive reaction backstage. Willow Nightingale will take on Toni Storm in singles competition next Wednesday on Dynamite. You can check out the lineup for the show by clicking here.
