John Legend and Chrissy Teigen Celebrated Their Daughter Luna's Birthday at Disneyland

Publish date: 2024-08-30
  • John Legend and Chrissy Teigen celebrated their daughter Luna's third birthday over the weekend at Disneyland in Anaheim, California.
  • Miles, the couple's 10-month-old son, posed for pictures with his big sister and parents during a princess-themed breakfast at Disney's Grand Californian Hotel.
  • Legend submitted himself and friend Mike Rosenthal for a feature on the Instagram page @dilfs_of_disneyland. The account spotlights "the hottest dads at Disneyland & California Adventure" theme park. After a last-ditch effort, Legend was featured on the account twice.

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend took a family trip to Disneyland to celebrate their daughter Luna's third birthday and the weekend-long festivities were fit for a Disney princess.

Luna looked precious dressed in a yellow gown inspired by Belle from Beauty and the Beast for a breakfast at Disney's Grand Californian Hotel. She also wore a pair of sparkly yellow sandals and her hair was in a topknot with the must-have accessory for every birthday toddler: a tiara. Along with her baby brother Miles, the family all posed with a cast of Disney princesses including Aurora from Sleeping Beauty and Tiana from The Princess and the Frog.

Handout//Getty Images

Handout//Getty Images

Both Chrissy and John shared content from their family trip on their respective Instagram accounts.

When they arrived at "the happiest place on earth," Legend posted a sweet photograph of Luna on his shoulders and Teigen grinning hard in the background. "Luna had a Disneyland birthday today! (As far as she knows, her birthday is today. Don't tell her it's Sunday please. Daddy has to work on Sunday. Thank you.)," he captioned his Instagram post.

In a second photograph, Legend posted a mild thirst trap with his friend and director-photographer Mike Rosenthal (husband of celebrity hairstylist Jen Atkin). Luna sat on top of her father's shoulders, while Miles chilled on Rosenthal's shoulders. "@mrmikerosenthal and I are submitting this to @dilfs_of_disneyland," Legend captioned the 'gram.

At the time, the Disneyland DILF account did not acknowledge Legend's post, so he went ahead and posted a second thirst trap. "This is my last, desperate attempt. Your move, @dilfs_of_disneyland," Legend captioned the picture of him pushing Luna in a stroller.

The following day, @dilfs_of_disneyland reposted not one, but both of John Legend's pictures to their feed. "John, how could we even make you wait this long... you are the definition of #strollermeat 🙌🏻 😍😍😍," they wrote in the first repost. The hot dads-focused account was so excited about the shoutout from Legend they wrote, "DOUBLE DILF spotting 😍😍😍" under the second regram.

Teigen posted a series of super cute photographs and videos to her Instagram from Lunda's birthday celebrations. "Happy birthday weekend, my loonie toons!!!," Teigen wrote under a video of Luna in a black and white swimsuit, sandals, and a pair of pink sunglasses.

The mother-daughter duo looked adorable as they sat on the edge of a hotel pool and splashed their feet.

After they spent some quality time lounging by the pool, they joined Teigen's mother for a family tradition. "My thai princess heading to the temple today (in the same bun crown I wore!) for thai New Year and her birthday blessing! also to get me some shrimp paste and pork rinds," Teigen wrote underneath the darling 'gram.

Last March, Luna joined her parents for a trip to Disneyland Hong Kong, and she thoroughly enjoyed her international trip. When she saw Minnie Mouse, she quickly embraced the Disney character with a big hug. However, Chrissy wasn't feeling the shade from her daughter. "She lets me hug her once a week," Teigen wrote under the heart-warming video.

Miles turns one in May, so we're hoping that our invite to the next Legend family birthday party doesn't get lost in the mail.
