The Legend of Oscar Robertson


The legendary figure in the basketball space Oscar Robertson, often referred to as "Big O", was a dynamic player in his career.. Additionally, his unique skills along with him being the first player in NBA history to average a triple-double throughout his career were enough reasons for fans and players alike to view as legendary. Robertson was born on November 24, 1938, in Charlotte, Tennessee, and started his impressive NBA career as a Rookie of the Year with Cincinnati Royals before playing for Milwaukee Bucks till he retired at the age of thirty-six.
