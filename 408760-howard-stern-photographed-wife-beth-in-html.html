Howard Stern's wife set social media ablaze following her stunning photos. Beth, 50, showed off her toned figure as she posed for Social Life Magazine in photos taken by her radio host husband.

Howard Stern's wife, Beth Stern, graced the cover of Social Life Magazine this month as the publication celebrated its 20th anniversary. The 50-year-old proudly showed off her ageless look in sexy bikini photos during the shoot.

The model, actress, author, and animal rights activist showcased a variety of swimsuits while striking poses on the beach with her famous husband acting as the photographer.

While chatting with the publication a decade ago, Beth Stern spoke about discovering her passion for helping animals. Beth and her husband got inspired following the loss of their bulldog, Bianca.

After Bianca's death, the couple decided to dedicate more time to helping Long Island's Bulldog Rescue, a non-profit that helps homeless bulldogs find foster homes.

The influential couple showcased their commitment by taking in a homeless bulldog as a foster and putting in considerable effort to locate a caring permanent home for the animal. Motivated by their rewarding involvement with the bulldog, the couple found inspiration to extend their fostering efforts to include cats and kittens.

Beth also candidly discussed her marriage with Howard, expressing her joy in having a partner who shares her deep affection and dedication to animals. She further revealed that her love for Howard grows stronger when witnessing his interactions with kittens and dogs.
