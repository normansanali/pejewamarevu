When did Michael Lockwood form his first band, Lions and Ghosts?

As Lockwood states, he worked in the 1980s with a band called Lions and Ghosts. The group signed with EMI America, a new record label that was a sister to Capitol Records, started by EMI.

The Lions and Ghosts discography included two albums:

  • Velvet Kiss, Lick of the Lime (1987)
  • Wild Garden (1989)

After their second album, Lions and Ghosts disbanded.

Lockwood has also been a music producer. He worked with Aimee Mann for nearly a decade. Other Grammy-winning artists who worked with him over the years included Carly Simon, Fiona Apple, and Susanna Hoffs of the Bangles.
