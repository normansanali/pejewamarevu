Is Eddie George the Best Ohio State RB Ever?

Publish date: 2024-07-24

By Jocelyn Alano July 01, 2024 18:05

credit. Fred Squillante / USA TODAY NETWORK

Eddie George, a name that Ohio State football fans hold in the deepest places of their heart. During his time at Ohio State, George was the definition of what a collegiate running back should be. It is not just the way his time in Scarlet and Gray aids him in being dubbed as one of the best running backs, if not the best all-time at Ohio State University. 


The Legacy of Eddie George


Eddie George's legendary path out of Ohio State was that of a great running back. He will forever be remembered in the annals of college football history, thanks to his years on the field and help guiding a team during crucial victories. His days terrorizing opposing quarterbacks, standout performances, and sheer dominance on the field have forever etched him into the very fabric of Ohio State football lore.


Unraveling the Impact

The on-field excellence of Eddie George has been a key part of his Ohio State legacy. In addition, his impressive stats and notable achievements such as having held a single-season rushing record of 1,927 yards and also being a one-time Heisman Trophy winner indicate the exceptional talent level he possessed at running back.


Transformative Influence

George transcends stats to make an impact. This elite duo helped Ohio State win several games, but perhaps more importantly they set a great example and hope for future athletes. His impact on the football program and those who came after him was immeasurable.


Enduring Legacy

His impact extends further than college football as well. All of this, combined with his successful changeover from college to the professional level and following achievements, further strengthens his identity as a football legend. For more than 60 years, his enshrinement in the College Football Hall of Fame and his lasting legacy in the professional game are testaments to that.


Unmatched Skill Set

Eddie George's agility, speed, and uncanny vision - especially in the open field makes him Ohio State one of the best running backs ever. With a game-awareness to go along with his play-making abilities, he is one of the best to ever don the scarlet-and-gray at running back.


Historical Significance

His place in history as a two-time Heisman Trophy winner and his contributions to the Ohio State Buckeyes' football tradition only make that title stronger for George. The on-field success and national reputation that Ohio State currently enjoys are based in large part on his pivotal contributions, which ultimately speak for the undeniable impact he had -- and continues to have -- within the program.
Enduring Influence
More than a collection of statistics and accolades, George's lasting impact on the football world in addition to his everlasting tie with both Ohio State brought forth visions that position him as nothing short of an icon of them all.
In Conclusion
Eddie George's incredible path from Ohio State to the National Football League and the lasting impact that he makes on those in the football community secure his standing among some of the Great RUNNING BACKS IN THE HISTORY OF OHIO STATE UNIVERSITY. His unparalleled ability, transcendent impact, and timeless legacy make him a figure that will withstand the test of time in college football lore; one cherished by Buckeyes fans everywhere and honored throughout the world.
Fitting into the grand tapestry of Ohio State football, Eddie George stands as a colossus - an unmovable determination; majestic talent, and enduring legacy that pushes you to make a case for his maybe being 1A (along with Archie) in terms of who was the best running back ever at THE OHIO STATE UNIVERSITY.
