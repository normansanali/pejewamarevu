Who plays young Rhaenyra in House of the Dragon?

In HBO’s House of the Dragon, Milly Alcock portrays little princess Rhaenyra. Is Milly Alcock related to Emma D’Arcy?

Who plays Meg in Upright?

Prior to House of the Dragon, Milly Alcock was best known for portraying Meg in the Australian drama Upright from 2019. The series tells the tale of two misfits who attempt to move a priceless piano from one half of Australia to the other and form an unexpected relationship in the desert.
