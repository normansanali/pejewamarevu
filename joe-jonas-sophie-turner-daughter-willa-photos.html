Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner Vacation in Miami with Daughter Willa

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Parents-of-two Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner spent a relaxing day with their eldest daughter this past Sunday.

The two were seen out on a walk in Miami with one-year-old Willa, who looked adorable in a little blue flower dress.

Turner held her mini-me in her arms during the outing, for which she looked casual in a relaxed, sporty outfit. The Game of Thrones star wore a long-sleeved deep green shirt over a pair of black biker shorts and stayed comfy in bright green shearling slides by Bottega Veneta. She wore no makeup and left her hair down, accessorizing only with yellow-tinted sunglasses.

Jonas, meanwhile, stayed cool in an elevated summer ensemble consisting of a striped green shirt with a large pink flower print, which he wore over a white tank top, paired with white shorts and white Adidas sneakers. He carried an olive green backpack and accessorized with black sunglasses and a pearl necklace.
