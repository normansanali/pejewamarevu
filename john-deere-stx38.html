Publish date: 2024-08-27

John Deere STX38 Specs, Price, Reviews, Transmission, Engine, Horsepower, History, Weight, Pictures

The John Deere STX38 Black Deck replaced the original John Deere STX38 yellow-deck model in 1994.

The John Deere STX38 black-deck model is considered to be an economy model with fewer features than the yellow deck.

John Deere STX38 Black Deck Lawn tractor is equipped with Kohler CV13S gasoline 1-cylinder air-cooled vertical-shaft, 398cc engine, which delivers 13 hp (9.7 kW) power at rated rpm 3600.

John Deere STX38 Black Deck Lawn tractor featured with 13HP Kohler 398cc 1-cyl gasoline engine, 4×2 2WD chassis, manual sector-and-pinion steering, integral mechanical disc brakes, and 5-speed gear.
