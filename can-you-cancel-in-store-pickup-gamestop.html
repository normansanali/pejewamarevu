Can you cancel in store PickUp GameStop?

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Yes, you can cancel in-store PickUp at GameStop. Simply contact the store that is holding the item and provide them with the necessary details. The store’s phone number can be found on your “Ready For Pick Up” email.

How do I cancel my in-store PickUp order at GameStop?

To cancel an order placed for in-store PickUp, you need to contact the store that is holding the item. You can find the store’s phone number on your “Ready For Pick Up” email. Simply call the store and provide them with the necessary details to cancel your order.

What happens if I don’t pick up my GameStop order?

If you don’t pick up a preorder at one of GameStop’s stores, they will sell your reserved copy to someone else. However, you will still receive a refund for the amount you put down towards the purchase of the preorder game.

Can I cancel my order at GameStop?

Yes, it is possible to cancel an order at GameStop. Depending on the processing cycle of the order, you may be able to cancel the order. To do so, click on the order number to review the order details and available self-service options. If available, you can update the credit card information, change the shipping service level, or cancel the order.

How long does it take for in-store PickUp orders to be ready at GameStop?

In most cases, GameStop will have your in-store PickUp order ready within two hours of purchase. The items will be held until the end of the current business day as long as the order was placed during store hours.

How do I cancel my GameStop order?

To cancel a GameStop order, you need to visit the GameStop website and go to your account. Alternatively, you can email [email protected] or call 1-800-883-8895 at least 5 days before your anniversary. If you choose to email your cancellation, make sure to provide your name and email address associated with your membership.

Will GameStop refund me if I return an item?

Yes, GameStop will refund your purchase if you return an item. You will need to return the item with all applicable components and original packaging. Items purchased in-store must be returned in-store. The refund will be issued to the original form of payment or a replacement will be provided, as indicated in GameStop’s return policy.

How do I cancel my same day delivery order at GameStop?

Canceling a same day delivery order at GameStop might not be possible in most cases, as GameStop aims to get items to you as quickly as possible. However, you can check if your order is eligible for cancellation by visiting your Order History on your Account Dashboard.

How does in-store PickUp work at GameStop?

To use in-store PickUp at GameStop, you need to select the items you want to buy and choose your preferred store. Complete the purchase process online and select the Pick Up At Store option. Wait until you receive a “Ready for Pick Up” email from GameStop. You can then contact the store when you arrive, and they will provide you with a contactless pick up for your items.

How long does it take for a GameStop order to be processed?

GameStop aims to process orders within 1 business day. After processing, the delivery time will depend on the shipping method you selected. It typically takes 2 business days to receive your order after processing.

Does GameStop charge for pre-orders?

GameStop will charge your credit card for a pre-ordered item when the item is processed and about 5 days before the release. This allows GameStop to get the product to you as quickly as possible.

How do I cancel an order after ordering?

To cancel an order after placing it, you need to contact the customer service of the website or store where you made the purchase. Make sure to provide them with your name, email, phone number, confirmation number, order items, order number, and a reason for the cancellation. Calling the customer service number provided on the confirmation email or order page is usually faster than email if the website has a 24/7 customer service line.

Are you allowed to cancel an order?

Under federal and state consumer laws, you are allowed to cancel certain contracts or sales of goods for any reason, including buyer’s remorse or even no reason at all. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) requires sellers to allow consumers a “cooling off” period in certain circumstances.

Can I return a game to GameStop if I opened it?

No, GameStop does not accept returns of opened products. The item must be returned in its original condition and packaging, and all applicable components must be included. GameStop’s return policy does not allow for returns of products that have been opened, played, damaged, or missing parts.
