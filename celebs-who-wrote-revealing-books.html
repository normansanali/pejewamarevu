10 Celebs Who Wrote Revealing Books

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Something that musicians like to do when they want to open up about what they are experiencing in their personal lives is to write down super emotional lyrics that allow fans to understand what they are going through. Musicians can express themselves through songs in such a creative way! But what do non-musicians do when they have stories to tell?

RELATED: Prince Harry And Meghan Markle Finding Freedom In New Tell All Book

They write tell-all books! Tell-all books usually always end up being successful because fans of celebrities want to know how celebs feel about certain things that took place in their lives whether it’s a scandal, a divorce, or something else.

10 'Around The Way Girl' By Taraji P. Henson

Taraji P. Henson is such an amazing actress with a long list of movies and TV shows to prove it. She also has a lot of awards to prove it as well but back in 2009, she lost to Penelope Cruz at the Oscar awards. In her book, she reveals Angelina Jolie is the one who consoled her while dealing with that particular devastation. Her book is filled with a lot more interesting stories like that one!

9 'Down The Rabbit Hole' By Holly Madison

Most people recognize Holly Madison from your time in the Playboy mansion. She was a Playboy bunny and actually one of Hugh Hefner’s girlfriends for a period of time. She released a book called Down the Rabbit Hole where she revealed that she contemplated suicide while living inside the Playboy mansion. Her book was released in 2015 and is filled with a lot of emotional details.

8 'Brunette Ambition' By Lea Michele

The phrase is usually “blonde ambition” but Lea Michele put a play on words with her book title. She called it Brunette Ambition and described what life was like for her on the set of Glee, the show that made her famous. New information has been revealed in recent days her costars have claimed that she was quite the bully on set but her book doesn’t shed much light on her. Instead, she talks about the fact that she at one point flashed her costars.

7 ‘A Life On The Edge’ By Carrie Fisher

In 2019, Carrie Fisher‘s book was released detailing her lifelong struggles with a few different major issues. She was dealing with addiction and bipolar disorder and her iconic career as an actress was impacted by that. Carrie Fisher played Princess Leia in the Star Wars movies and passed away in 2016 which means that her book was released three years after her tragic passing.

6 ‘Through The Storm: A Real Story Of Fame & Family In A Tabloid World’ By Lynne Spears

Britney Spears and Jamie Lynn Spears's mother, Lynn Spears, decided to release a book about what life was like for her family being constantly scrutinized by the media and the public. She wrote about everything from Britney's career to Britney's public mental breakdown. She revealed details about how the entire family was affected by Britney Spears's high-level of fame and the fact that she was a pop-culture icon.

5 ‘Elvis & Me’ By Priscilla Presley

Priscilla Presley wrote a tell-all book about her relationship with Elvis Presley that detailed the many scenarios that they experienced together. Parts of the book got very explicit as she revealed that he pursued her when she was still a teenager and had her doing things for him that were inappropriate for her being an underage girl.

She also talked about his use of illegal substances, the fact that he was having multiple affairs with other women, and his jealousy issues.

4 ‘The Most Beautiful: My Life With Prince’ By Mayte Garcia

When Prince passed away, it was absolutely devastating. People in the 80s were obsessed with his music and thought he was one of the most talented individuals ever. He was highly skilled when it came to singing, songwriting, and playing instruments. He was in a relationship with a woman named Mayte Garcia at one point in time and she wrote a love story about everything she experienced in her relationship with him while he was alive.

3 'Open Book' By Jessica Simpson

Jessica Simpson was super open and honest about past relationships in her autobiographical book. She talked about the details of her marriage and divorce to Nick Lachey which a lot of people wanted to know about after they start on a reality TV show together.

She also describes the details of her relationship with John Mayer which has been described as messy by people on the outside looking in.

2 'The Last Black Unicorn' By Tiffany Haddish

Tiffany Haddish is known for being a hilarious comedian and actress Who has worked with a lot of other A-list celebrities including Kevin Hart in the past. In her revealing book, she talks about childhood abuse that she endured and she also describes the fact that she was able to find her voice by doing stand-up comedy. She obviously went down the right path because her career is an awesome place.

1 'Believe Me' By Yolanda Hadid

Yolanda Hadid is the mother of Gigi Hadid and Bella Hadid, two of the biggest models alive in 2020. Yolanda Hadid used to be a model herself back in the day. She starred on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and in her book, she describes what happened to her marriage with David Foster and talks about what life was like with her reality TV costars.
