Who Is Evin Ahmad?

Evin Ahmad is a Swedish actress of Kurdish origin. She was born in Kista, Stockholm, Sweden, on June 8, 1990. She studied at the Stockholm Academy of Dramatic Arts and made her screen debut in 2007 with a film adaptation of Jonas Hassen Khemiri’s novel Ett Öga Rött (One Red Eye).

Ahmad has since appeared in a number of films and television series, including The Rain, Quicksand, and Snabba Cash. She has been nominated twice for the Guldbagge Awards, Sweden’s equivalent to the Academy Awards, for her roles in Dröm Vidare (2018) and Ring Mamma! (2020). In 2022, she was named Shooting Star at the Berlin International Film Festival.

Ahmad is known for her versatility and her ability to portray complex and nuanced characters. She is a rising star in the Swedish film and television industry and is sure to continue to achieve great things in her career.
