Biography

Ezra Olubi (born 12 November 1986) is an accomplished entrepreneur in the IT sector.

He is the co-founder of Paystack, a leading online payment platform in Nigeria alongside Shola Akinlade.

After its acquisition by a prominent International payment system (World Stripe), the award-winning software developer, Ezra Olubi amassed a net worth of $100,000,000.

He also worked as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Jobberman and Delivery Science.

Early Life

Ezra Olubi was born on the 12th of November, 1986 in Ibadan, Oyo State.

He grew up in the South-western part of Nigeria. At some period of time, the Yoruba-speaking prodigy exuded a noticeable level of brilliance and was known by many in his neighborhood.

He went ahead to become one of the leading entrepreneur in the IT sector alongside his close friend, Shola Akinlade.

Education

Upon completion of his secondary school education, Ezra Olubi got admitted into Babcock University to study Computer Science, graduating in 2006 with a Degree in the discipline.

He was also an award-winning computer programmer during his time in the university.

Career

Ezra Olubi worked as an IT administrator at Business Management Consultant Limited, after graduating from Babcock University. Not long after, North Ocean Logistic and Solutions Limited reached out to him and appointed him as a Web App Developer under its firm.

He was also the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Jobberman and Delivery Science before being made the Director of Magic at Alexander Haring Limited.

Ezra Olubi teamed up with Shola Akinlade to establish PayStack, an online payment platform in 2015. However, his big break came when World Stripe, an accomplished online payment system, reached out to the duo to acquire PayStack in October 2020.

The deal saw Ezra Olubi and Shola Akinlade rack up the sum of $200,000,000 for their platform and launch them into limelight in the IT sect.
