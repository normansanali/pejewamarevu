Mike Beltran- Professional Career

Mike started his referee journey firstly by engaging in boxing in the year 2002. He is a mixed martial artist who began training in boxing at the Azteca Gym in Bell during his early days, He holds a brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

His career journey started in 2009 when he started refereeing MMA matches alongside some of the famous MMA refs including Herb Dean, and Big John McCarthy. In addition, during his 10 years career, he has officiated some of the mega bouts such as Nate Diaz vs.

Josh Thomson, and Jon Jones Vs. Alexander Gustafsson and many more. Further, he also replaced Herb Dean in the office when he suffered an injury for the 232 heavyweight title bout.

In the year 2017, he was elected as referee of the year and is a winner in the general election on November 6, 2018, with 55.27 percent of the vote defeating Democrat Debbie Katt.
